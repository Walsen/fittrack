## Overview
Titan Athletic Complex is a 25,000 sq ft multi-purpose training facility catering to athletes, bodybuilders, and general fitness members. It features dedicated zones for strength, cardio, functional training, and sports performance. The gym also includes a turf area for agility and sprint work.
