# Flex Fitness Center

## Location
456 Oak Avenue, Midtown

## Hours
Open 24/7

## Overview
Flex Fitness Center is a modern, 24-hour gym designed for general fitness enthusiasts. With a balanced mix of cardio, machines, and functional training areas, it suits beginners through intermediate athletes. The facility covers 12,000 sq ft and includes a dedicated stretching zone.
